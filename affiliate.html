Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to commonly asked questions
what transformation can the student expect to see at the end?

Our goal is that all participants will gain confidence in their ability to plan, execute and replicate a successful launch. This will allow service providers to offer a high-quality service to their clients and charge higher ticket prices for their skills. Business owners will leave with a better understanding of the tech, strategies and skills needed to develop a successful funnel and lead their team through a launch.

what's unique about this program?

This is not a coaching program or a DIY course. This is a combination of technical trainings, guided accountability + support and proven processes and tools that will provide a new skillset for participants.

WHO IS BLACK FRIDAY BOOTCAMP FOR?

There are two distinct groups of women this program is for. Firstly, it is designed by service providers for service providers who need training on how to deliver a specific service as part of their offer. These women should have participated in at least one prior launch and have existing clientele. They have most likely experienced difficulty managing clients, struggled with knowing how to build a specific launch timeline and felt burnt out after managing a chaotic launch. This will mostly include Virtual Assistants and OBMs.

The second group of women is comprised of individual business owners who have a proven offer or product that they need help launching. These women have already launched this offer, at least once, and have either felt like it was a "failure" or have hesitated to launch again after their prior experience.

What does the program curriculum look like?

Black Friday Bootcamp is a 9-week program. It will kick off on 10.4 and run through 12.6. Each week will be built around a specific skill or stage of the launch process and will include resources, templates, videos, homework and a live call. There will be two weeks of dedicated co-working time to allow students to implement the trainings into building and launching. There will also be a dedicated Slack channel for participants to ask questions, receive accountability prompts and celebrate wins!

who is leading the black friday bootcamp program?

The program was co-created and will be co-hosted by Cierra Robbins and Jordyn Bailey. These two women are industry leaders when it comes to sales strategy, launch management and tech integration. They have helped clients bring in 2+ million in revenue, over the last year alone, through 100+ launches together.

How much will it cost to register?

Members of the Black Friday Bootcamp waitlist will be offered a $200 discount between 8.19-9.20 as well as Early Bird registration. Their total cost will be $1997 and payment plans will be available. Non-waitlist members will have the opportunity to sign up between 9.20-10.3 for $2197. They will also have payment plans available.

when are live calls and what if i can't attend one?

Due to the expected size of each cohort in the program, we know that it will be difficult to accommodate everyone's schedule to one single date or time. We will have a one-hour call each week. If students are unable to attend this live call, they will have access to the live replay within 48 hours of the call. They will have access to this replay as long as the program exists.

What will registrants have access to during the program?

Registrants will be invited to join the Black Friday Bootcamp membership area, inside Kartra, by Monday 10.4. This is where all resources, tools, trainings and templates will be shared on a weekly basis. Registrants will also be invited to join the Black Friday Bootcamp Slack channel and a recurring calendar invite for the weekly live one-hour call.

will cierra + jordyn be managing each person's launch?

Nope! Cierra and Jordyn will offer consultation, training and support for each registrant. However, they will not have access to or hands on management of any specific launch.
